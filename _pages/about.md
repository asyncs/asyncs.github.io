I'm an M.S. student in the [Computer Engineering Department](https://w3.cs.bilkent.edu.tr) at [Bilkent University](https://w3.bilkent.edu.tr/bilkent/), studying Task and Motion Planning (TAMP) under the guidance of [Dr. Ozgur S. Oguz](https://oz-oguz.github.io). As a member of the [Learning for Intelligent Robot Assistants Lab (LIRA)](https://bilkent-lira.github.io), my research focuses on enhancing the intellectual capacity of embodied agents.

While high-level task planning has been extensively studied, my work addresses the often-overlooked challenges of motion planning and its seamless integration with higher-level reasoning. Inspired by evolutionary biology, where motor skills preceded higher intelligence, my research emphasizes developing frameworks that allow embodied agents to adapt to dynamic environments, efficiently use available information, and generalize their behavior. By combining task-and-motion planning with an understanding of environmental affordances, I aim to enable agents to interact more intelligently and effectively with their surroundings.

I hold a B.S. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from [Bilkent University](https://ee.bilkent.edu.tr/en/), with a strong emphasis on machine learning and robotics.

For a comprehensive overview of my professional journey, please refer to my [CV](assets/pdf/YenicesuCV.pdf). Feel free to [email me](mailto:sarp.yenicesu@bilkent.edu.tr); I’m always happy to discuss research and collaboration opportunities.
