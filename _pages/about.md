I hold an M.S. in [Computer Engineering](https://w3.cs.bilkent.edu.tr) from [Bilkent University](https://w3.bilkent.edu.tr/bilkent/), where I specialized in Task and Motion Planning (TAMP) under the guidance of [Dr. Ozgur S. Oguz](https://oz-oguz.github.io) as part of the [Learning for Intelligent Robot Assistants Lab (LIRA)](https://bilkent-lira.github.io). My research focused on enhancing the intellectual capacity of embodied agents by tackling the integration of motion planning with high-level task reasoning.

Inspired by evolutionary biology, my work emphasized developing adaptable frameworks for embodied agents to interact intelligently in dynamic environments. By merging task-and-motion planning with environmental affordances, I aimed to enable agents to generalize behaviors and utilize information more effectively.

I also hold a B.S. in [Electrical and Electronics Engineering](https://ee.bilkent.edu.tr/en/) from [Bilkent University](https://w3.bilkent.edu.tr/bilkent/), with a strong foundation in machine learning and robotics. Always open to research and collaboration opportunities, feel free to connect or reach out to me for further discussion.
